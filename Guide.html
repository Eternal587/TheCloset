There are several types of chromebook exploits that are all useful depending on what your situation is.

Proxies
A method of getting around an extension by loading the website through a server then to your own chromebook one of the most basic exploits

Extension Removers/Editors
Removing or disabling a chrome extension that blocks the use of websites deemed unfit for school or websites that they just don't like

Kiosk Explots
Kiosk Exploits are exploit that open a unblocked browser by abusing a kiosk app. One example is Skiovox which uses the fact that you can open up a settings menu thats opened in a chrome tab whenever your wifi is cut off in a Kiosk app

Custom OS's
Running a Custom OS isn't that difficult and all it is is just abusing ChromeOS Shims. ChromeOS Shims are recovery files used to recover your chromebook from recovery mode but an be abused to run just about any OS by editing the Shim.

Unenrollment
Unenrollment is really the Ultimate Exploit and what it does is it well does what its called. It unenrolls your chromebook in the schools policy allowing you to use the chromebook as if it were your own. Custom accounts, Changing Settings, ect.
